### What is solidity ?

- is an object-oriented and high-level language for implementing Smart contracts.
- Solidity is statically typed and supports inheritance, libraries, and complex user-defined types among features.
- This features enables to create contracts for users such as voting, crowdfunding, blind auctions, and multi-signature wallets.
